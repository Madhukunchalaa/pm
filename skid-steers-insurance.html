What Insurance Do We Offer?

With Plant Machinery Insurance, you can find a vast list of skid steer insurance policy options, which can also be customized as per your needs. Our personal skid steer insurance or business skid loader insurance offerings include:

Skid Steer Equipment Insurance

Comprehensive coverage protecting your skid steer from accidental damage, theft, and breakdowns.

Liability Insurance

Safeguard against third-party claims for property damage or bodily injury caused by your skid steer's operation.

Equipment Breakdown Coverage

Insurance for mechanical failures, ensuring your skid steer is shielded from unexpected repair expenses.

Rental Reimbursement Coverage

If your skid steer is out of commission due to an accident or repair, this coverage can help with rental expenses for a temporary replacement.

Cargo Insurance

If your skid steer is used to transport materials or equipment, cargo insurance can cover damages to the transported items during transit.
